Founder

Dr. Connor Robertson

Entrepreneur. Author. Builder.

Dr. Connor Robertson founded Seymour Maison with a single conviction: that a t-shirt, made properly, is among the most refined objects a person can own. After years building businesses across real estate, tax advisory, and publishing, he turned his attention to what he wore every day and found it wanting.

The Seymour Maison project began quietly in 2019 as a private pursuit, the obsessive documentation of everything that makes a shirt extraordinary. The sourcing trips. The atelier relationships. The hand-feel comparisons that filled three notebooks. It became a label because Connor believed others deserved access to the same quality he had spent years chasing.

Dr. Robertson holds a doctorate in business and has built, advised, or invested in more than a dozen companies across North America. He lives between Pittsburgh and wherever the next acquisition takes him.

"Ownership is the answer to most of life's questions. That principle applies to your portfolio, your business, and your wardrobe."
Dr. Connor Robertson
